Cappuccino

Rp 25.000

Cappuccino is a type of coffee beverage that is made from espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. The ratio of espresso to steamed milk to foam is usually 1:1:1, giving the drink its distinctive flavor and texture.

Americano

Rp 20.000

Americano is a type of coffee drink made by diluting espresso with hot water. Americanos are often served in a standard coffee cup and are a popular alternative to traditional drip coffee.

Espresso

Rp 18.000

Espresso is a type of coffee beverage made by forcing pressurized hot water through finely ground coffee beans. It is known for its strong, bold flavor and concentrated form.

Mocha

Rp. 23.000

The name "Mocha" has become synonymous with a type of coffee beverage made with espresso and chocolate syrup.

Coffee Latte

Rp 25.000

A latte is a type of coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk. They are a popular choice for coffee drinkers looking for a creamy, comforting and flavorful pick-me-up.

Matcha Latte

Rp 27.000

A matcha latte is a type of green tea-based beverage made with matcha powder, steamed milk, and sweetener. Matcha lattes are known for their unique flavor, which is slightly sweet and bitter, also a slightly grassy taste.
